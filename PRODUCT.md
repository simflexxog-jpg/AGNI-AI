# PRODUCT

Name: AGNI AI
Description: A web-based conversational assistant UI with a polished dark dashboard, chat composer, suggested prompts, and Google sign-in.
Platform: web
Register: product
Audience: knowledge workers, creative professionals, and teams who want a sleek AI chat workspace.

## Goals
- Provide a modern AI chat dashboard with history, model selection, and settings.
- Offer a secure login experience with Google authentication.
- Surface helpful prompts and fast access to AI interactions.
